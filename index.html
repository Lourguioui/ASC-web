Algeria Startup Challenge ?


Algeria Startup Challenge is a program that aims to boost project leaders with startup ideas in the form of innovative solutions to improve the daily lives of Algerian consumers and the development of the collaborative economy.

Event page

Boosting the entrepreneurial ecosystem within the framework of the valorisation of
strategic and future sectors as factors of attractiveness and innovation of startups.

Create a bridge between higher education institutions
and the professional world.

Facilitate the emergence of a new generation of entrepreneurs open to the world
and to technological development.


Bringing together the different actors necessary to create an
ecosystem that benefits everyone.


Putting project leaders to the challenges of the research and development problems of SMEs.

Preliminary stage

  • A national program that brings together project leaders from across the country.

  • 10 to 15 projects will be pre-selected by the scientific committee in each wilaya, which will have to compete by presenting a pitch in order to seduce the jury and bring 3 projects to the finals.

Finale stage

    WorkshopDay :

  • A conference on the latest news on the entrepreneurial scene in the Algerian market.
  • A series of workshops "the entrepreneur's must-have toolbox".

    PitchDay :

  • Opening conference and presentation of the different actors present.
  • Exhibition and stands of the different organizers present.
  • Pitching of the winning projects coming from all over the country in order to seduce the jury and win the grand prize in each category.
  • The result : One winner in each category.

CapCoWork is an incubator dedicated to the creation of an ecosystem.
Innovative entrepreneurial company whose dynamic is based on the promotion of and the coaching of Algerian youth with the support of the local economic actors.
Its mission is to :

  • Participate in the emergence of an ecosystem favorable to the development of innovation while encouraging the exchange of good practice. local level, regional and international.
  • Creating spaces for meeting and exchange between young people project leaders, companies, public authorities, universities and Entrepreneurs
