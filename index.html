Algeria Startup Challenge ?


Algeria Startup Challenge is a program that aims to boost project leaders with startup ideas in the form of innovative solutions to improve the daily lives of Algerian consumers and the development of the collaborative economy.

Event page

Boosting the entrepreneurial ecosystem within the framework of the valorization of
strategic and future sectors as factors of attractiveness and innovation of startups.

Creating a linking bridge between higher education institutions
and the professional world.

To facilitate the emergence of a new generation of entrepreneurs who are open to the world
and to technological development.


Bringing together the different actors in order to create an
ecosystem that benefits everyone.


Developing project leaders exposing them to the challenges of research and development problems of SMEs problems.

E-commerce and Fintech

Circular economy and renewable energy

Preliminary stage

  • A national program that brings project leaders from across the country together

  • 10 to 15 projects will be pre-selected by the scientific committee in each wilaya, which will have to compete by presenting a pitch in order to seduce the jury and bring 3 projects to the finals.

Final stage

    WorkshopDay :

  • A conference dedicated to the latest news about the entrepreneurial scene in the Algerian market.
  • A series of workshops ''the entrepreneur's must-have toolbox''.

    PitchDay :

  • Opening conference and presentation of the different actors.
  • Exhibition and stand/booths of the different organizers.
  • Pitching of the winning projects coming from all over the country in order to seduce the jury and win the Grand Prize in each category.
  • The result : One winner in each category.
